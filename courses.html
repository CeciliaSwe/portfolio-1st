Dog Leadership

This is a leadership course for dog owners based on motivation, love and demands; it is not a dog training course. We will give you the tools you need to build a strong and long lasting relationship with your dog.

Puppy Training

Take the chance to give your relationship journey a head start! Socialise your puppy in a controlled environment, learn soft leadership techniques and build a healthy relationship from the start.

Kid & Dog

The same philosophy as always, but taken down to a level that suits the child's relationshipwith the dog. Utilizing play as the main tool to respectfully build on the special bond between child and dog.
