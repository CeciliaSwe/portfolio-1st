Our Philosophy

At Playful Paws we do not train dogs, we train people. We believe that leadership can be improved at all levels and that there are no "bad dogs" - just misunderstood ones. Join our community and improve your relationship with you four-legged best friend!

About us

We have trained dog owners for almost 2 centuries now, and we love it as much as when we started. Our 3 dogs set the example of both dos and don'ts and we are delighted to share our knowledge with you! Checkout "Courses" to see what fits you the best!
